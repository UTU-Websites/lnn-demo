Rugano

  • RŪGANO RWA MŪTIGA-IRĪ
    Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge
  • Gūciarwo
    Mūtiga-irī Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge aaciarirūo 28th November 1970 iturainī rīa Rironi arī mwana wa keerī wa Richard Njoroge Nyanjui na Margaret Wambūkū Njoroge. Maciaranīirūo na Salome Wambūi Nyanjui, Irene Nyamathwe Mwangi, Rose Wangūi Nyanjui na Winnie Mūthoni Karanja.
  • Ūnyinyi Wake
    Mūtiga-irī Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge nīaateithagia aciari ake mawīra ma mūciī ota ciana iria ingī cia mahinda mau. Aarī mwana mwathīki, wi kīoo na mwendi thaayū.
  • Githomo
    Mūtiga-irī Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge ambirīirie gīthomo gīake cukuru-inī wa Tigoni Primary mwaka-inī wa 1977 nginya mwaka-inī wa 1986 rīrīa arīīkirie kīraathi gīa kanaana. Thutha ūcio nīaathiire cukuru wa Ngenia High School na agīcooka agīthiī Mūrūria Secondary School kūrīa arīīkirie kīraathi gīa form four mwaka-inī wa 1990. Thutha ūcio nīacookire agīthiī college būrūri-inī wa India kūrīa aathoomire Degree ya Bachelor of Commerce.
  • Kūrua
    Mūtiga-irī Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge nīaruire kūri mwaka wa 1986 na agīthiī nambere na mathomo make.
  • Kanitha
    Mūtiga-irī Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge niatūarirūo nī aciari ake kanitha-inī wa P.C.E.A Rironi arī mwana mūnini na agībatithio. Niacookire akīigīrirūo moko mwaka-inī wa 1984. Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge aingīrire kanitha wa A.C.K St Pauls Rironi mwaka-inī wa 2001 oimīīte kanitha wa P.C.E.A Mother church. Nīaigīrīirūo moko ni Mūūthikaabu mūhuurūku wīra Peter Njenga thuutha wa kurīīkia kīrathi. Nīaacookire agīthuurūo gūtuīka ūmwe wa Kīama gīa Kanitha mwaka-inī wa 2002 na agīkorwo arī mūigi kīgīīna kwa ihinda rīa matīīna meerī nginya mwaka wa 2006.
  • Wīra
    Mūtiga-irī Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge nīambīrīirie wīra kambuni-inī ya General Accident Insurance. Nīarutīīte wīra wa insurance na Kenyan Alliance. Thuutha ūcio nīathiire wīra United Arab Emirates, PETANNS driving school na kūndū kūūngī kūingī.
  • Kūhikania
    Mūtiga-irī Lawrence Nyanjui nīmaacemanirie na mwendwa wake Jane Nduta mwaka-inī wa 2000 na makīhikania mwaka-inī ūcio kanitha-inī wa ACK St. Lukes Sigona. Nīmaarathimirūo na mwana wao waa mbere nīwe Mitchelle Wambūkū Nyanjui mwaka-inī wa 2001. Thuutha ūcio nīmaatanahīirūo na mwana wao wakeerī Shirley Wairimū Nyanjui mwaka-inī wa 2006.
  • Kūhurūka
    Mūtiga-iri Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge nīakoreetūo ena ūgima mwega wa mwīīi mīaka yake yoothe. Kūri 25th December nīacemanirie na mūtino wa Barabara na akīhuurūka. Aahuurūka ena ūkūrū wa miaka 54. Nīatiga mūtumia wake Jane Nduta Nyanjui na ciana ciake igīrī. Nī mwathani waheeanire Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge noowe wacokera, rīītwa rīake rīroogocūo.

Eulogy

  • EULOGY OF THE LATE
    Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge
  • Birth
    The late Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge was born on 28th November 1970 in Rironi village as the second born to Richard Njoroge Nyanjui and Margaret Wambūkū Njoroge. His siblings are Salome Wambūi Nyanjui, Irene Nyamathwe Mwangi, Rose Wangūi Nyanjui, and Winnie Mūthoni Karanja.
  • Childhood
    The late Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge in his childhood used to help his parents with various home chores just like his peers. He was obedient, hardworking and peace-loving.
  • Education
    The late Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge started his education at Tigoni Primary School in the year 1977 until 1986, when he sat for his KCPE exam. He then progressed to Ngenia High School and then proceeded to Mūrūria High School, where he sat for his KCSE exam. After high school, he went to India for his college education, where he studied the Bachelor of Commerce Degree.
  • Initiation
    The late Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge was initiated in the year 1986 and thereafter continued with his education.
  • Church
    The late Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge was introduced to Christianity in his infancy at PCEA Rironi Church, where he was baptized. In the year 1984, he was confirmed in the same church. Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge joined ACK ST. Paul’s Church Rironi in the year 2001 from PCEA mother church. He was confirmed by Bishop Emeritus Peter Njenga after completing confirmation class. He was later elected to join the church council in the year 2002 where he served as a treasurer for two terms until 2006.
  • Career
    The late Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge started his career at General Accident Insurance. He also worked with Kenya Alliance Insurance. Later he worked in the United Arab Emirates, PETANNS Driving School among many other companies.
  • Marriage
    The late Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge met the love of his life, Jane Nduta, in the year 2000, and they got married in the same year at ACK St. Lukes Church Sigona. They were blessed with their firstborn daughter, Mitchelle Wambūkū Nyanjui, in the year 2001. Later they were blessed with their second born daughter Shirley Wairimū Nyanjui in the year 2006.
  • Demise
    The late Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge has enjoyed good health in his entire life. On the evening of 25th December 2024, he was involved in a road accident where he met his demise. He died at the age of 54 years. He has left behind his beloved wife, Jane Nduta Nyanjui, and his two daughters.
    It is the lord who gave us Lawrence Nyanjui Njoroge and it is the lord that has taken. May his name be praised.
